Pushpa : The Rise (2021)

2021 Highest Grossing Film

The film became the top earner in India for the year of 2021.

300 Crore Club

Allu Arjun's first movie to gross ₹300 crores worldwide.

International Appeal

"Pushpa: The Rise" became the first South Indian film in 2021 to hit the $2 million mark in the United States.

Surpassing Bollywood Releases

The Hindi dubbed version performed exceptionally well and achieved pan-India popularity.

Opening Day Milestone

Pushpa 2 achieved a record-breaking opening day collection of ₹270 crore globally.

Hindi Version Success

The Hindi version alone earned ₹793.2 crore, surpassing Baahubali 2 and Jawan.

India Net Collection

Fastest Indian cinema to cross ₹1,234 crores.

Global Gross

The film collected ₹1,871 crore worldwide, making it the second highest-grossing Indian film after Dangal.

Highest Footfall Post-Pandemic
