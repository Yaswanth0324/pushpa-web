Pushpa Movie Series (Pushpa: The Rise & Pushpa 2: The Rule)

The Pushpa movie series is an action-packed Indian Telugu-language film series directed by Sukumar, starring Allu Arjun in the lead role. The series is known for its raw storytelling, intense action, and gripping performances.

1️⃣ Pushpa: The Rise (2021)

Director: Sukumar

Producer: Mythri Movie Makers & Muttamsetty Media

Release Date: December 17, 2021

Languages: Telugu (Dubbed in Hindi, Tamil, Malayalam, and Kannada)

Budget: ₹170-200 crore

Box Office Collection: ₹365-400 crore (worldwide)

OTT Release: Amazon Prime Video

Plot Summary

Set in the dense forests of Andhra Pradesh, Pushpa Raj (Allu Arjun) is a coolie (laborer) who works in the illegal red sandalwood smuggling business. Despite being an ordinary worker, his intelligence and ambition help him rise in the ranks, making enemies in the process.

Highlights

  • Allu Arjun's iconic performance with the "Thaggede Le" (I won’t back down) attitude.
  • Mass action sequences & raw storytelling.
  • Devi Sri Prasad's blockbuster songs like Oo Antava, Srivalli, and Saami Saami.
  • Climax Scene: A powerful face-off between Pushpa and Bhanwar Singh, setting up the sequel.

2️⃣ Pushpa 2: The Rule (2024)

Director: Sukumar

Producer: Mythri Movie Makers

Release Date: August 15, 2024 (tentative)

Languages: Telugu, Hindi, Tamil, Malayalam, Kannada

Budget: Estimated ₹350-400 crore

Story Expectations

The sequel, Pushpa 2: The Rule, will continue Pushpa Raj’s story after he establishes his dominance. The plot is expected to show:

  • Pushpa’s Rise to Kingpin: His war against both the police and the mafia.
  • Bhanwar Singh's Revenge: The police officer will go to extreme lengths to bring Pushpa down.
  • Political Angle: Pushpa may enter the political scene to gain more power.
  • Bigger Action & More Intensity.

Confirmed Highlights

  • Allu Arjun's new, intense look from the teaser.
  • "Where is Pushpa?" Mystery—he was rumored to be dead but makes a grand return.
  • Bigger action sequences, stunts, and drama.

Pushpa Franchise Impact

  • Allu Arjun won the National Award for Best Actor for Pushpa: The Rise.
  • "Thaggede Le" became a viral trend.
  • Music by Devi Sri Prasad was a global hit.
  • The Hindi version became a surprise blockbuster, competing with Bollywood films.
